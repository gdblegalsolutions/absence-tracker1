How to Use This Tool

  • Enter your UK entry date and visa decision date (if different) in the fields above
  • Set your planned application submission date - you can change this to find the best time to apply
  • Add all periods when you were absent from the UK using the table below
  • The tool automatically calculates if you meet the SET(O) absence requirements
  • If you entered the UK after your visa decision, that period is automatically counted as an absence

SET(O) Absence Requirements

  • No more than 180 days absent in any rolling 12-month period during the 5-year qualifying period
  • No more than 540 days absent in total during the entire 5-year qualifying period
  • Both requirements must be met to be eligible for settlement
  • The 5-year period is calculated backwards from your application submission date
