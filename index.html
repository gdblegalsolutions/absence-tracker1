SET(O) Absence Requirements

For your SET(O) application to be successful, you must meet BOTH of these requirements:

  • No more than 180 days total absence during the 5-year qualifying period
  • No single absence longer than 90 consecutive days

Qualifying Period: 5 years immediately before your application date
